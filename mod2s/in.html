Our Menu

Chicken

Usually, even when chicken is the centerpiece of a spread, it's just kind of there—a source of lean, healthy protein with a timid flavor—more a vehicle for sauce than a star on its own. But it doesn't have to be that way. Chicken can be bold and flavorful. Whether working with the whole bird or its parts, you have the potential to turn the humble chicken into a meal that can go toe-to-toe with pork, beef, or game.
